Pre Exercise

Exercise Duration Recommended carbohydrate
3-4 hours before exercise

  • Pasta with tomato sauce, cheese/lentils and vegatables
  • Rice with cheese/lentils and vegatables
  • Chicken or prawn stir fry with noodles
  • Sandwich with chicken/fish/cheese/egg/falafel/peanut butter and salad
30-60 mins before exercise
  • Banana or other fresh fruit
  • Handful of fruit and nuts or seeds
  • Smoothie
  • Fruit and nut bars
  • Oat or granola bars
  • Porridge, cereal or wholegrain cereal with milk

During Exercise

Exercise Duration Recommended carbohydrate Type of carbohydrate
<45 mins None
45-75 mins Very small amounts (mouth rinse)

  • Raisins - 40g
  • Medjool dates - 2
  • Energy Bar
  • Energy Gel
>2.5 hours Up to 90g per hour Multiple transportable types. (Pick three of above per hour)
